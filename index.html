Technology

How It Works

01

Temperature Detected

The NTC sensor reads your drink's temperature every few seconds and feeds data to the microcontroller.

02

Brain Processes

The ESP32 / Arduino Nano processes the temperature data and checks your last-drink timestamp.

03

Face Reacts

The OLED display renders a live animated face — happy when you drink, worried when you haven't, showing exact temp.

04

You Stay Hydrated

Timely reminders and friendly nudges keep you on track with your daily hydration goals — effortlessly.
