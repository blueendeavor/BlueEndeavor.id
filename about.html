Our Story

Born From a Simple
Idea

What if your tumbler could tell you when your water was too hot — and remind you to drink?

The Beginning

From Tinkering to
Product

Blue Endeavor started as a late-night experiment — an Arduino Nano, a tiny OLED screen, an NTC sensor, and a question: can we make a tumbler feel alive?

The first prototype had a blinking face that smiled when you drank and looked sad when you forgot. We showed it to friends. They laughed — then immediately asked where they could buy one.

That was the moment Blue Endeavor was born. We refined the hardware, improved the firmware, upgraded to ESP32 for richer features, and started building tumblers that people genuinely connect with.

Our first prototype, 2024
